RICE / STATEMENT / NEW ORLEANS

Severe and welcoming. Spare but alive with thought.

01 / REGION

The Gulf South is not a backdrop.

It is infrastructure, weather, labor, language, devotion, extraction, and a set of pressures still operating in the present tense.

02 / FORM

We resist inherited softness.

RICE is informed by post-alt-lit, C86 zine culture, retro modernism, and work that treats form as an ethical decision.

03 / OUTWARD

Local does not mean enclosed.

We publish writers rooted in or connected to the Gulf South while looking outward to broader cultures, technologies, and movements.

04 / PRESENT

Nostalgia is not the only archive.

We value writing that embraces the present moment, experiments with form, and refuses to make the region harmless.
