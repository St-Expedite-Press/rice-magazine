Send previously unpublished work with a brief cover note and third-person bio. We care more about the work than the performance of literary professionalism around it.

We are drawn to formal risk, precise regional knowledge, contemporary pressure, strange humor, documentary residue, and sentences that do not ask permission to become difficult.

Email submissions@ricejournal.com with the genre and title in the subject line.
