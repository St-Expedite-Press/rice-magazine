Crawfish Pond with Saints

Crawfish net lines tangled in saints, cold diesel in the pump house air, mosquitoes hum a hymn for labor, and water rises everywhere. At dusk the traps become small altars. Each red float keeps its place. The field receives the last machinery without forgiveness, without grace.
C. Sandbatch

8 lines
